Academic Organizer V.1

Universities are chronically bad at displaying the information you need to know about your own grades, this is my solution, use at your own peril.

Features

- Track assignment completion and grades.

- See course stats: weighted grades, assignments remaining, and min/max grades.

- To-do list, for tracking tasks and their due dates.

- See the soonest task due, and how many tasks are left to be completed.

- Sort and filter tasks based on categories.
